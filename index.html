Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

Do you provide print-ready files?

Yes. All exports are CMYK with correct bleed and margins. We can also package source files if needed.

What apps do you use?

Adobe Photoshop and CorelDRAW for layout, image prep, and final exports. We follow prepress best practices.

How many revisions are included?

Two rounds are included on most projects. Additional iterations are available at a simple hourly rate.

Can you add my product photos and copy?

Absolutely. Share assets via Drive or WeTransfer and we'll integrate them into the layout.

Can you match our brand guidelines exactly?

Yes. Share typography, colors, spacing, and any do/don't rules. We'll build master styles from them.

Do you support multi-language layouts?

We recommend language-aware styles and flexible text frames. We've shipped English + Arabic and English + Hindi editions.

What's your revision workflow?

We use PDF comments or shared boards for markup. Each round is tracked so stakeholders see changes clearly.

Do you provide source files?

On request, yes. We package fonts (if licensing allows), links, and assets with clear folder structure.
